…
Chime at the top of every hour
Interrupt instead of pause/resume
Unticked (default): the show pauses for the chime and resumes where it left off. Ticked: the chime cuts the track off and the show does not resume.
Per-hour recordings are assigned in the classic view's Hour Chimes editor.
